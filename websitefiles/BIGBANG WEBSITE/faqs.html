What does Big Bang Exports specialize in?

Big Bang Exports specializes in exporting high-quality agricultural and mineral products such as spices, jaggery, millets, tea, coffee, cashew nuts, quartz, granite, and more.

Where are your products sourced from?

Our products are ethically sourced from trusted farmers, cooperatives, and mines across India, ensuring authenticity, quality, and sustainability in every shipment.

Do you export globally?

Yes, we export to multiple countries across the globe. Our logistics network and documentation support make international delivery seamless and on time.

What quality assurance measures do you follow?

All our products go through rigorous quality checks, including grading, packaging inspection, and compliance with international export standards to ensure premium quality.

What is the typical lead time for orders?

Lead time depends on the product type and destination country, but typically ranges from 10 to 30 business days, including processing and shipping.

Can I request customized packaging or private labeling?

Absolutely. We offer customizable packaging and private labeling services to meet your brand's needs and market standards.

How do you ensure timely deliveries?

With a strong logistics infrastructure and experienced supply chain partners, we maintain a 98% on-time delivery rate across all product categories.

Do you support bulk orders?

Yes. We are equipped to handle large-scale bulk orders with consistent quality, timely delivery, and complete export documentation.

Are your agricultural products organic?

Many of our products are organically grown, and we are continuously working with farmers to adopt and expand organic farming practices.

How can I become a partner or distributor?

We welcome partnerships worldwide. Simply reach out through our contact page or email us, and our business development team will connect with you to explore collaboration opportunities.
