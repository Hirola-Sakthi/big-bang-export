Product

Copra

Big Bang Exports is a trusted exporter of premium-quality copra from India, known for delivering top-grade dried coconut kernels ideal for coconut oil extraction and various culinary applications. Our copra is sourced directly from carefully managed coconut plantations, where coconuts are handpicked at optimal maturity to ensure the highest oil yield and quality.

Natural Processing & Quality Assurance

Our copra undergoes traditional sun-drying methods that help preserve its natural nutrients, oil content, and flavor profile. The kernels are then meticulously cleaned and graded to guarantee purity and compliance with international food safety standards. We supply both ball copra and edible copra variants to suit diverse industrial needs.

Applications & Markets

  • Coconut oil manufacturers rely on our copra for its high and consistent oil content.
  • Cosmetic and personal care industries use our copra as a base for natural oils and skincare products.
  • Wholesale food suppliers and exporters value our copra for its reliability and premium quality.

Our commitment to quality and sustainability makes us a preferred partner for global buyers seeking authentic Indian copra.

Choose Big Bang Exports for your copra needs and experience premium products delivered with reliability and trust.
