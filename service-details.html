Jaggery & Jaggery Powder

Big Bang Exports is a leading supplier and exporter of premium-quality jaggery and jaggery powder. Our jaggery products are sourced from certified organic farms and are processed using traditional, chemical-free methods. This ensures that all the natural minerals like iron, magnesium, and potassium are retained. Our jaggery is available in solid block and fine powder forms, making it ideal for cooking, baking, and health-based food preparations. Known for its rich taste and deep aroma, our jaggery is perfect for customers looking for a healthier substitute for refined sugar. We supply bulk jaggery and jaggery powder for food industries, grocery chains, and international importers.
